Duck Breast with Carrot Purée

Description

A gourmet main course featuring seared duck breast with crispy skin and tender meat, served over a silky carrot purée subtly flavored with butter and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 duck breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 large carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup cream or milk
  • Optional: pinch of cumin or ginger for purée
  • Fresh thyme (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Score duck skin in a criss-cross pattern and season well.
  2. Place skin-side down in a cold skillet and cook on medium until fat renders and skin is crisp.
  3. Flip and cook 3–4 minutes more for medium rare; let rest.
  4. Boil carrots until tender, then blend with butter, cream, and spices until smooth.
  5. Slice duck breast and serve over warm carrot purée. Garnish with thyme.
