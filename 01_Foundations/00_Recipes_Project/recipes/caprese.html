Caprese Salad

Description

A fresh and vibrant Italian salad made with ripe tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil, drizzled with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella on a plate.
  2. Tuck basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately as a starter or side dish.
