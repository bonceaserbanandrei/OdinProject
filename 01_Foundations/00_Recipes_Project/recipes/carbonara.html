Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

An authentic Roman pasta made with just five ingredients: eggs, Pecorino Romano, guanciale, black pepper, and spaghetti â€” creamy without using any cream.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g guanciale (or pancetta as substitute)
  • 3 large egg yolks + 1 whole egg
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, for pasta water

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Cut guanciale into small strips and cook over medium heat until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks, whole egg, cheese, and pepper until smooth.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving 1/2 cup of cooking water.
  5. Add pasta to guanciale, mix well off heat.
  6. Quickly stir in egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed to make it creamy.
  7. Serve immediately with more Pecorino and black pepper.
