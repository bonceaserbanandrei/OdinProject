Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish that combines spaghetti with sautéed garlic, olive oil, chili flakes, and parsley for a simple yet delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and chili flakes; sauté until garlic is golden.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving 1/4 cup of the cooking water.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti and reserved water to the pan.
  6. Toss well to coat the pasta with the garlic oil.
  7. Add parsley and serve immediately with optional Parmesan.
