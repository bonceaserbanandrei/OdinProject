Couscous with Salmon

Description

A light and nutritious meal featuring fluffy couscous, pan-seared or baked salmon, and fresh vegetables or herbs for a balanced, quick dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 cup hot water or vegetable broth
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chopped parsley or mint (optional)
  • Cherry tomatoes or cucumbers (optional)

Steps

  1. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  2. Pan-sear or bake salmon at 180°C (350°F) for 12–15 minutes.
  3. Place couscous in a bowl, pour over hot water or broth, cover and let sit 5 mins.
  4. Fluff couscous with a fork and mix in olive oil, herbs, and chopped veggies.
  5. Serve salmon on top of couscous with extra lemon juice or herbs.
